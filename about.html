Our story

Marketing that
means something

Lochside Creative is a social media and creative agency built for ambitious service-based businesses who want more: more bookings, more confidence, more visibility, and more freedom.

We help you grow your business with content, branding and strategy that actually works — so you can stop guessing, stop blending in, and finally show up online as the leader you are.

We built Lochside Creative for business owners who want to grow with intention, not burnout. You didn't start your business to spend your evenings posting on Instagram, second-guessing every caption, or trying to DIY your brand identity. You started it for freedom. To do what you love. To make an impact. To build something meaningful. And that's exactly where we come in.

Meet the team

Meet
Molly

Molly is a marketing expert with years of hands-on social media experience. As a Gen Z business owner, she grew up with social media — starting micro-influencing at 16, which is where she discovered her passion for creating content that actually connects.

She's worked with a huge range of service-based businesses — from dentists and decorators to law firms, coaches, and hair salons — helping them build a strong presence online. She built her entire first business completely organically, without ads. Just strategy.

Co-founder

Meet
Owen

Owen's background isn't the typical creative route. After serving in the Royal Marines, creativity was always his outlet — drawing, tattoo design, skateboarding. Over time, that passion evolved into graphic design and branding.

He studied graphic design for 18 months and realised he could combine creativity with strategy to make a real impact for businesses. At Lochside Creative, he helps brands transform how they're seen — building identities that position you as the go-to in your industry and attract the right clients effortlessly.
